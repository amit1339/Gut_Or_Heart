✨ Key Features

🎴

Swipe & Predict

No complex odds. Just swipe left or right to make your predictions with fun visual cards.

👥

Social Gaming

Create teams with friends and family. Compete against each other on the leaderboard.

🎨

Colorful UI

Bright, cheerful design that's nothing like traditional betting apps. Confetti on wins!

💰

Pool Rewards

Optional shared pool system. Winner takes most, runner-up gets second, everyone stays happy.

⚡

Quick & Easy

Make 4 quick swipes per day. No complex calculations or betting experience needed.

🌍

For Everyone

Perfect for casual fans, families, and office groups. Soccer knowledge? Optional.
